B.S. in Math/Stats from Purdue University, Masters in Ops Research/Information Engineering from Cornell University. My current interests include Natural Language Processing and its applications, optimization of machine learning methods, and time series forecasting.

Projects

  • Retro Analytics

    Scrapes historical prices of old video games on ebay and posts/comments from Reddit. With this data, we train an ARIMA model on the historical data to see which games are likely to increase in value. We also pair these forecasts with general sentiment of the game in online communities.

  • Chinese Tutor

    I am currently developing a personal Chinese tutor trained on my chinese textbooks. The first phase is generating quality data from the textbooks by accurately extracting the English, pinyin, and Chinese characters. Since the scanned version of the textbook had a healthy amount of imperfections, I used OpenAI's GPT API to correct the text by parsing small chunks and combining the final result. The current phase is implementing retrieval augmentation for the Chinese tutor agent.
